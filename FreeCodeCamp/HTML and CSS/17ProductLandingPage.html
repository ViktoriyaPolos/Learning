About

Vik's Chocolate is a premium chocolate brand that prides itself on blending the finest ingredients with a touch of artistry to create a truly exceptional chocolate experience. Founded by master chocolatier Viktoriya Polos, the brand has a rich heritage of chocolate-making spanning over three generations. With an unwavering commitment to quality, Vik's Chocolate aims to delight chocolate connoisseurs with its exquisite creations.

Our Process

At Vik's Chocolate, every step of the chocolate-making process is meticulously executed to ensure unparalleled quality and taste. The process begins with the careful selection of cocoa beans sourced from sustainable and ethical farms around the world. These beans undergo a thorough inspection and are roasted to perfection to enhance their flavor profiles. Once roasted, the cocoa beans are meticulously ground to a fine paste called chocolate liquor. Vik's Chocolate takes pride in its traditional stone grinding method, which imparts a velvety smoothness to the chocolate. The chocolate liquor is then tempered to achieve the ideal texture and consistency.
